In the game, Aegis is a relic of the Titan War, once wielded by Zeus and Athena and now reduced to a chaotic, semi-alive fragment. Its nature is unstable: not a defensive tool but a violent force. The historical shield it resembles is a round bronze sheet from the kingdom of Pontos, carrying a star pattern and the name of King Pharnakes I. Although it looks impressive, it was made from very thin metal and was probably used in ceremonies rather than real combat.
In simple terms, both shields were created to represent power as much as to protect.
