This website is the final project for the 'Information Modeling and Web Technologies' course at the University of Bologna, led by Prof. Fabio Vitali. The main goal is to explore how different designs—specifically typography and layout—can be used to improve the digital experience of museums. Crucially, we investigate how effective storytelling can be applied when moving museum collections online. The documents presented here were chosen for their complexity from various public sources; they are used strictly for this academic exploration and are not intended to replace the original publications.
